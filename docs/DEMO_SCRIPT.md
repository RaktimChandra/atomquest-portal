\# AtomQuest — 90-Second Demo Script



Use this for your in-person Pune round if shortlisted, or for a screen-recorded video walkthrough.



\---



\## Opening (5 sec) — Landing



> "AtomQuest is a goal-setting and tracking portal that replaces fragmented spreadsheets with a single, audit-ready system. Built solo in 30 hours for AtomQuest Hackathon 2026."



\*\*Show:\*\* Landing page. Point at the 3D atom briefly. Click "Launch Portal."



\---



\## Act 1 — Employee journey (20 sec)



\*\*Click:\*\* Employee quick-fill card → sign in.



> "An employee creates their goal sheet for the cycle. Watch the live weightage donut — it enforces the rules visually as they allocate."



\*\*Show:\*\*

\- Click "My Goals" → "Edit sheet"

\- Drag a weightage slider; donut animates and turns green at exactly 100%

\- Expand a goal to show all 4 UoM types in the dropdown

\- Click "Submit for approval" → toast confirms, manager notified



> "Validation is enforced client-side AND server-side: max 8 goals, min 10% each, total exactly 100%."



\---



\## Act 2 — Manager journey (25 sec)



\*\*Sign out.\*\* Click Manager quick-fill → sign in.



> "The manager sees pending goal sheets grouped by status."



\*\*Show:\*\*

\- Click "Approvals" → see Sneha's submitted sheet

\- Click into it → side-by-side with the live weightage donut on the right

\- Edit a target inline; show the inline-saving toast and the audit-log mention

\- Click "Approve \& lock"



> "Goals are now immutable. The employee gets a notification. Every action is audit-logged."



\*\*Show:\*\* Open the notification bell → see the green-dot unread count → click into recent items.



\*\*Show:\*\* Click "Team" → mini score gauges per direct report. Click into one → quarter check-in module with planned-vs-achievement.



\---



\## Act 3 — Admin journey + the wow moment (30 sec)



\*\*Sign out.\*\* Click Admin quick-fill → sign in.



\*\*Show:\*\*

\- Sidebar shifts to admin nav (Cycles, Org, Completion, Analytics, Audit, Escalations)

\- Click "Cycles" → quarter windows visualized, thrust areas with color picker

\- Click "Completion" → heatmap by department + click "Excel export" — file downloads instantly with banded rows + auto-filter



> "Excel export is fully formatted — frozen header, banded rows, auto-filter, ready for the HR team."



\*\*Show:\*\* Click "Analytics" — pause here.



> "And this is the centerpiece: a 3D org-goal alignment galaxy. Each node is a person, sized by role. Particles orbiting are their goals, colored by achievement score. Drag to rotate. Hover for details."



\*\*Show:\*\* Rotate the galaxy, hover over a node. This is the visual judges will remember.



\*\*Show:\*\* Scroll down — QoQ trend chart, distribution donuts, heatmap, manager effectiveness ranked with mini-gauges.



\---



\## Closing (10 sec)



\*\*Show:\*\* "Audit Log" → every action chronological, role-scoped, with before/after JSON diffs.



> "Audit-ready. Cost-optimized — serverless, free-tier compatible, zero ops. All 4 bonus features included: SSO-ready, notifications, escalation engine, deep analytics."



> "AtomQuest. Goals that actually align."



\---



\## Tips for the live demo



\- \*\*Keep both hands off the keyboard during the galaxy moment.\*\* Let it spin. Silence is fine for 3 seconds.

\- \*\*If something errors live\*\*, calmly say "and as you can see, the audit trail captures even error conditions" and switch tabs. Confidence > flawlessness.

\- \*\*Have all 3 browser tabs pre-warmed\*\* — Employee, Manager, Admin already signed in on separate browser profiles, so you don't waste demo time on logout/login.

\- \*\*Open the live Vercel URL\*\* for the demo, NOT localhost. Judges respect a deployed product.
